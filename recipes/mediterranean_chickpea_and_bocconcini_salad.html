Mediterranean Chickpea and Bocconcini Salad

Description

My favourite salad, it's perfect for summer barbeques. Can be made with or without croutons depending on the number of calories desired. Best eaten warm, but can also be enjoyed cold.

The recipe written serves 2. Ingredient amounts don't need to be exact for salads. For more servings, use entire packages of perishable ingredients from the grocery store (within reason) and combine according to taste.

Ingredients

  • 398 mL Chickpeas
  • 100 g Bocconcini Cheese
  • 7 g Basil
  • 113 g Baby Spinach
  • 15 mL Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1 Avocado (optional)
  • 113 g Baby Tomatoes
  • 28 g Sunflower Seeds
  • Garlic
  • Ciabatta Roll (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to a high broil. Start preparing the recipe when the oven is ready. Meanwhile, wash and dry all produce. Halve tomatoes. Tear the bocconcini into bite-sized pieces. Halve, pit, peel and cut avocado into 1/2 inch pieces. Peel and mince, or grate garlic.
  2. Prepare 2 foil lined baking sheets. Drain and rinse chickpeas. To a large bowl, add the chickpeas and 1 tbsp oil. Season with salt and pepper, then toss well to coat. Transfer to the first preparred baking sheet. Broil on the lower rack of the oven, for 14-16 min,stirring halfway through cooking, until tender and golden in spots.
  3. Meanwhile, tear ciabatta into 1-inch chunks. To the second preparred baking sheet, add ciabatta, garlic and 1 tbsp oil. Season with salt and pepper, then toss to coat. Broil on the middle rack of oven, for 4-5 minutes, until the croutons are crisp.
  4. Meanwhile, to the same bowl (from step 2), add balsamic vinegar and 2 tbsp oil. Season with salt and pepper, then whisk to combine.
  5. To the bowl with the dressing, add chickpeas, tomatoes, avocado, and spinach. Toss well to coat, Divide chickpea salad between plates. Top with the bocconcine, croutons and sunflower seeds. Tear the basil and sprinkle over top. Enjoy!
