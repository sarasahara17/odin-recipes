Mediterranean Chickpea and Bocconcini Salad

Description

My favourite salad, it's perfect for summer barbeques. Can be made with or without croutons depending on the number of calories desired. Best eaten warm, but can also be enjoyed cold.

The recipe written serves 2. Ingredient amounts don't need to be exact for salads. For more servings, use entire packages of perishable ingredients from the grocery store (within reason) and combine according to taste.

Ingredients

  • 398 mL Chickpeas
  • 100 g Bocconcini Cheese
  • 7 g Basil
  • 113 g Baby Spinach
  • 15 mL Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1 Avocado (optional)
  • 113 g Baby Tomatoes
  • 28 g Sunflower Seeds
  • Garlic
  • Ciabatta Roll (optional)
